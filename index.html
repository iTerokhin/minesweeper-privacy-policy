Privacy Policy

App: Minesweeper Y
Last updated: December 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use Minesweeper Y (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Overview

  • The App does not require user accounts to play.
  • You can play the App without providing personal information.
  • Optional analytics and feedback features use third-party services.

2. Analytics (TelemetryDeck)

The App uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics service, to understand how the App is used and to improve gameplay and stability.

TelemetryDeck processes:

  • Anonymous usage events (e.g., feature usage, app interactions).
  • Basic device and app information (such as app version and operating system).

TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, does not track users across apps, and does not collect personally identifiable information.

TelemetryDeck Privacy Policy: https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy

3. Information you may provide (Feedback & Comments)

When using the optional feature voting and feedback section, you may choose to provide:

  • Comment text (free-form text you submit).
  • Name or nickname (optional; can be any text and does not need to identify you).

Providing a name is optional. You are encouraged not to submit real personal information.

4. Feature voting & comments (Votice)

The App uses a third-party service called Votice to collect feature votes and comments. When you use this feature, data is transmitted to Votice and processed according to their privacy practices.

Votice may process:

  • User-submitted content (comments, feature requests, votes).
  • Optional display name or nickname provided by the user.
  • Device and app information (device type, operating system, app version).
  • Anonymous usage data for service reliability and analytics.

Comments and feedback submitted through Votice may be visible to other users within the feedback interface.

Votice Privacy Policy: https://www.votice.app/privacy.html

5. How information is used

  • To improve gameplay and app performance.
  • To collect and prioritize feature requests.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse of feedback systems.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only:

  • With TelemetryDeck for anonymous analytics.
  • With Votice to provide the feedback and commenting feature.
  • When you choose to submit content visible to other users.
  • If required by law or to protect legal rights and safety.

7. Data retention

Analytics and feedback data are retained according to the policies of the respective service providers. You should avoid submitting sensitive or personal information in comments.

8. Children’s privacy

The App is not intended to collect personal information from children. Users should not submit personal data in comments. If you believe personal information has been submitted unintentionally, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

9. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent version.
